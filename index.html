Introduction

I am a programming instructor with a background in engineering and physics. My special interest is in modeling physical and social phenomena, and I have recently taken up business process modeling with Simio.

While I was teaching my students about visual and object oriented programming, I have been inspired -and frequently taught- by them about the wonders of web applications. I intend to develop my own web applications in the near future.

My most important work is a WPF-based class/control library which will -hopefully- be used by students and researchers to develop computer-based psychology experiments. This library is a part of my HurPsy project which is housed on GitHub. HurPsy also includes a WPF Windows application which will help non-programmers to design psychology experiments.
